METHODOLOGY

Transparent, not prophetic

Current strength is measured using model seed ranking. History combines World Cup titles and best-ever finishes. Experience uses tournament appearances, with a limited advantage applied to the three hosts. Match outcomes use Elo-style probabilities and a draw baseline, then repeat through Monte Carlo simulation.

Limitations: Injuries, final lineups, live form, player-level data, and betting markets are not included. Results are exploratory model outputs, not betting advice.
